What We Do
Our Capabilities

Advanced machining, fabrication, and welding — all under one roof with industry-leading equipment.

01
CNC Machining

Milling, turning, boring, and precision machining of complex components to tight tolerances. From one-offs to production runs.

02
Welding & Fabrication

CWB certified welding and custom steel fabrication. Structural, structural, MIG, TIG, and stick welding for industrial applications.

03
Plate Rolling

Steel plate and pipe rolling for cylinders, cones, and curved sections of any diameter. Precision forming for pressure vessels and structural components.

04
Plasma Cutting

High-definition plasma cutting for precise profiles in steel plate and structural shapes. Fast, accurate, and ready for downstream fabrication.

05
Heavy Fabrication

Multiple overhead cranes and up to 10-ton lifting capacity. We handle large, heavy structures that other shops can't.

06
Custom Parts & Repairs

Prototype, reverse-engineer, or repair components to exact spec. We work with clients to fully understand requirements before a single cut is made.
