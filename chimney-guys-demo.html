What We Do
Our Services

From annual chimney sweeps to full fireplace installations — we handle it all under one roof.

🧹
Chimney Sweeping & Repair

Annual cleaning removes dangerous creosote buildup. We also handle tuckpointing, relining, cap installation, and structural repairs.

🔵
Gas Fireplace Services

Installation, conversion, cleaning, and repair for all major gas fireplace brands. We can also help you choose the right model for your home.

🪵
Wood Fireplaces & Stoves

Wood-burning fireplace and insert installation, maintenance, and WETT inspections. Freestanding units and custom masonry available.

⚡
Electric Fireplaces

Beautiful, zero-maintenance electric fireplace units for any room. We supply and install linear, traditional, and built-in electric models.

🍖
BBQ & Outdoor Fire Pits

Extend your living space outdoors. We supply and install BBQ grills, outdoor fireplaces, and fire pits for year-round entertaining.

🏠
Custom Fireplace Renovation

Full makeovers including framing, tile work, and classic masonry. Traditional, contemporary, or modern — we'll match your vision exactly.
