What We Do
Our Services

From sweeping your chimney to a full custom fireplace install — we handle everything.

01
Chimney Sweeping & Repair

Regular chimney cleaning removes dangerous creosote buildup. We also handle tuckpointing, relining, cap installation, and full structural repairs.

02
Gas Fireplace Services

Gas fireplace installation, conversion, cleaning, and repair. We service all major brands and can advise on the best model for your home.

03
Wood Fireplaces & Stoves

Wood-burning fireplace and stove installation, maintenance, and WETT inspections. We carry inserts, freestanding units, and custom masonry options.

04
Electric Fireplaces

Beautiful, zero-maintenance electric fireplace units for any room. We supply and install linear, traditional, and built-in electric models.

05
BBQ & Outdoor Fire Pits

Extend your living space outdoors. We supply and install BBQ grills, outdoor fireplaces, and fire pits for year-round entertaining.

06
Custom Fireplace Renovation

Full fireplace makeovers including framing, tiling, masonry, and finishing. Traditional, contemporary, or modern — we'll match your vision.
