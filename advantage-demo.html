About Us
Edmonton's Trusted Welding Company

Advantage Manufacturing Ltd. operates as a one-stop shop for steel products, offering construction, repair, portable, and welding services in Drayton Valley, Edmonton, and surrounding Alberta.

With over a decade of experience handling stainless steel and aluminum, we've continuously improved our techniques and facilities. We're approved by the Canadian Welding Bureau — meeting and exceeding industry standards.

We don't just create or repair — we collaborate. We discuss designs thoroughly with you and address every concern before we begin.
