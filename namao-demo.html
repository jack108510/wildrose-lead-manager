About Us
Family Owned Since 1972

For over 50 years, Namao Auto & RV Repair has been locally owned and operated by the Suranyi Family — serving Sturgeon County, Edmonton, St. Albert, and surrounding areas.

As a second-generation small business, we take the time to explain every job in advance. Our technicians are trained to repair your vehicle or RV right the first time — no matter the age, shape, or size.

We also have a courtesy vehicle available for reservation when your vehicle is in the shop.
