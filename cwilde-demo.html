02

Meet the Designer
Carmen Wilde

Carmen Wilde, lead designer at C. Wilde Designs Inc., has honed her skills over the past 30 years working on a wide range of residential and commercial projects across Alberta.

With a passion for design and a commitment to professional growth, Carmen has stayed at the forefront of industry trends. Her focus on green design elements and creating healthy living and working environments has inspired countless remarkable spaces.

"Great design isn't just about how a space looks — it's about how it makes you feel when you're in it."

Carmen is also an accomplished painter whose deep connection to nature is reflected in her vivid original works — available for sale or rent.

How It Works
Our Design Process

A clear, collaborative approach from first conversation to final reveal.

01
Discovery

We start with a conversation — understanding your space, your lifestyle, your goals, and your aesthetic preferences.

02
Concept & Planning

We develop a design concept with mood boards, space plans, material palettes, and a detailed project scope.

03
Design Development

Finalizing selections — furniture, finishes, fixtures, lighting, and art — and coordinating with suppliers and trades.

04
Reveal

We manage installation and the final styling so your space is complete, cohesive, and exactly as envisioned.
