Who We Are
A Community of Collectors

The Wild Rose Antique Collectors Society has enriched Edmonton's collector community for 50 years. Our vibrant group of over 50 passionate individuals thrives on a shared love for history, antiques, collecting, and engaging outings.

Antiques, in our perspective, aren't just relics of the past — they resonate with the contemporary, reminding us that nothing in design or fashion truly starts from scratch. For us, treasure hunting is about those delightful eureka moments.

Our legacy includes heritage donations and proactive contributions to projects, museums, and charities across Alberta.
