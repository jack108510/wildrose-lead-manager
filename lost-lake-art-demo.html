The Experience
More Than a Workshop.
A Full Retreat.

Lost Lake Art Weekend takes the traditional painting workshop and puts it somewhere extraordinary. You're not in a studio — you're at a private lakeside estate, surrounded by nature, with nothing to do but create.

Guided by a professional instructor, you'll work through technique, composition, and expression over two immersive days — with meals, accommodations, and the full estate at your disposal.

Your Instructor
Meet the Instructor
Willie Wong
Edmonton Fine Artist & Art Teacher

Willie Wong is one of Edmonton's most beloved fine art instructors — a classically trained painter with over 20 years of teaching experience. Originally from Malaysian Borneo, Willie settled in Edmonton in 1979, where the natural landscape became the new subject of his art.

Known for his work in watercolour, acrylic, and oil, Willie has a rare gift for making technique accessible to all levels — from complete beginners to experienced artists. He teaches with patience, depth, and genuine enthusiasm for the craft.

Willie has been a fixture in Edmonton's arts community for decades, exhibiting at The Works, the Edmonton Fringe Festival, and teaching at Kamena Gallery & Frames. The Lost Lake Art Weekend is a rare opportunity to spend a full weekend learning from him in an extraordinary setting.
