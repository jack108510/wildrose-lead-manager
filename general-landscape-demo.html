What We Do

Complete Property Care,
Every Season

Professional landscaping and maintenance services for residential and commercial properties across Edmonton.

Lawn Maintenance

Regular mowing, edging, trimming, and fertilization to keep your lawn healthy, green, and manicured all season long.

Spring & Fall Cleanup

Thorough seasonal cleanups including leaf removal, bed clearing, debris hauling, and property prep for the next season.

Tree & Shrub Services

Expert pruning, shaping, and trimming of trees and shrubs to promote healthy growth and enhance your property's curb appeal.

Concrete Curb Edging

Clean, durable concrete curbing that defines garden beds and borders, adding a polished, professional look to any landscape.

Grading & Drainage Repair

Proper land grading and drainage solutions to protect your foundation, prevent pooling water, and redirect runoff away from structures.

Landscape Design & Install

Full landscape design, installation of garden beds, planting, mulching, and hardscaping to transform your outdoor living space.

Fence & Deck

Quality fence and deck installation, repair, and staining. Built to Alberta's climate standards for lasting strength and style.

Pressure Washing

High-pressure cleaning for driveways, walkways, decks, fences, siding, and patios to restore surfaces to like-new condition.

Foundation Waterproofing

Protect your home from moisture damage with professional exterior waterproofing, drainage tile, and membrane installation.

Snow & Ice Removal

Reliable winter property maintenance including snow plowing, shoveling, and ice control for driveways, walkways, and commercial lots.
