The Artist
Carmen Wilde

Carmen Wilde is an Edmonton-based landscape artist with a deep affinity for the natural world. Her work is inspired by the Canadian wilderness — lakes, forests, mountains, trails, and coastlines — capturing the essence of wild places through colour, light, and texture.

With over 30 years of experience as a professional artist and interior designer, Carmen brings both technical mastery and a deep emotional connection to every piece.

"Art is how I process the world — every painting is a conversation with nature."
