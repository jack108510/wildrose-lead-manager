What We Do

Complete Electrical Services

From routine residential work to complex commercial installations — VA Electric handles it all with the precision of a master electrician.

Panel Upgrades

Modernize your electrical panel for safety and capacity. We handle full upgrades, replacements, and sub-panel installations to meet today's demands.

Emergency Repairs

Electrical issues don't keep business hours. We're available when you need fast, reliable emergency repair service to restore power and safety quickly.

Residential

New builds, renovations, and upgrades for your home. Outlets, lighting, circuits, hot tubs — we handle every residential electrical need.

Commercial & Warehouse

Full electrical for commercial spaces, warehouses, and shops. We understand the demands of industrial environments and meet them to code.

Fire Alarm Systems

Installation and maintenance of fire alarm systems for residential and commercial buildings. Stay compliant and protected with certified solutions.

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 EV charger installation for homes and commercial properties. Future-proof your property with a professional, permitted EV charging setup.

LED Retrofit & Lighting

Upgrade to modern, energy-efficient LED lighting for homes, offices, warehouses, and retail spaces. Cut energy costs with a professional retrofit.

Garages & Custom

Dedicated garage circuits, workshop wiring, custom electrical for unique projects. We bring master-level expertise to jobs of every shape and size.
