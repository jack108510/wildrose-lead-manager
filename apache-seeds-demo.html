Our Story
Edmonton's Garden Centre Since 1958

Apache Seeds Limited has been a fixture in Edmonton's gardening community for over 65 years. We're locally owned and operated, and we're proud to be the go-to source for everything from backyard vegetable gardens to commercial growing operations.

Our knowledgeable staff are passionate about plants and gardening. Whether you're a first-time gardener or an experienced grower, we're here to help you succeed — from seed selection to harvest.
