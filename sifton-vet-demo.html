About Us
Northeast Edmonton's Trusted Veterinary Clinic

Sifton Park Veterinary Clinic has been building relationships with pet families in Northeast Edmonton for over 15 years. We're devoted to providing the highest quality of care to our patients, while supporting pet owners in a caring, family-oriented atmosphere.

Our goal is to empower owners through knowledge and compassionate care — providing guidance throughout your pet's life, with a focus on prevention as well as detection and treatment of illness.

We are proudly locally and independently owned.
