About Us
Red Deer's Pressure Wash Experts

Ma-Shine Pressure Wash & Steaming Services was founded over a decade ago with a focus on top-quality pressure cleaning. Being a fully mobile service, we operate 24/7 and can work around your business hours.

Our specialized high-pressure equipment reaches 3,000 PSI at up to 425°F — powerful enough to tackle oilfield equipment, grease, graffiti, and heavy industrial build-up. We do both wet and dry cleanups depending on the job.
