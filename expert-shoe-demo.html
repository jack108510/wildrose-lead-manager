Our History
A Family Tradition Since 1955

Expert Shoe Clinic has been serving Edmonton for over 70 years — a story of Italian craftsmanship, hard work, and generational pride.

1955
Pat DeLuca immigrates from Italy, bringing generations of shoe-making expertise to Edmonton.
1961
Pat partners with "Pop" Kostiuk at Expert Shoe Clinic on 9922–101 Avenue.
1962
Pat becomes sole owner and opens a second location at 10316–100 Street.
1982
Luigi DeLuca, Pat's son, joins the family business at age 11 — 4th generation in the trade.
Today
Luigi leads Expert Shoe Clinic at 10020–103 Avenue, continuing 70 years of Edmonton shoe repair excellence.
