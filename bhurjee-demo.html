Our Story
Nearly 30 Years of Precision

Founded in Edmonton in 1997, Bhurjee Industries has grown from a local machine shop into a global precision manufacturing partner.

1997
Dave and Jas Bhurjee launch Bhurjee Industries, serving the Edmonton oil & gas sector.
1999
Acquired first CNC lathe — establishing Bhurjee as a highly responsive production machining shop.
2008
Moved to a 10,000+ sq ft facility in Edmonton's industrial district, anchoring a world-class supply chain.
Today
Delivering precision manufacturing solutions to clients in Canada, the United States, and the Middle East.
